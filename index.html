Program overview

Dry leases built around flight-school utilization.

Pricing can be shown publicly by aircraft and tier. Examples include unlimited hours for a fixed monthly price, or a 50-hour monthly minimum with hourly overages.

If the exact aircraft is not listed, we may be able to source or acquire additional Cessna 172S aircraft for qualified long-term operators.

Typical lease framework

  • Monthly minimum or unlimited-hour pricing options
  • Overage rate for hours above included hours
  • Security deposit based on aircraft and operator profile
  • Maintenance, insurance, hangar, and records obligations defined in the lease
  • Monthly Hobbs/Tach reporting and payment process
